This EnGenius EnShareTM app lets you monitor and manage your EnGenius Cloud Router or upload and download video, music, and other files to and from a USB-connected hard drive to the router wherever you have an Internet connection.

 

With EnShare, you can:

+ Monitor the network traffic on your Cloud Router

+ Remotely disconnect computers, mobile or other devices connected to your Cloud Router

+ Upload files back to your connected USB hard drive when traveling

+ Access and download video, music and other files when you're away from home
