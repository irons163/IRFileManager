Free EnShareTM service enables users to create an EnGenius Cloud for accessing and sharing content from anywhere using iPhone®, iPad® and AndroidTM-based smartphone and tablets. 

 

To create an EnGenius cloud via EnShare, simply attach a USB storage device to the EnGenius IoT Gateway/Intelligent Router to allow users to share media and other files.

Storage. Documents and multimedia (music, video and photo) files in your USB storage device attached to your EnGenius device can easily be accessed in the home or away from home on mobile devices such as smartphones, tablets or laptops.

Collection. Enables users to manage similar file types together. With the Upload function, users can upload photos from their mobile device to the attached USB storage device.

Status. Monitor and manage the status of all uploads/ downloads at a glance.

Router. Allows users to remotely monitor and manage their home networks with the ability to check traffic status, manage Internet access of computers and devices attached to the router, and even reset security settings as needed when they are away from home.

About. General and legal information, FAQs.
